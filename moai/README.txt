SETUP:
1. Put the 'moai' file with no extension in your Assets folder.
2. Put the 'Moai.dll' in your Mods folder.

HOW TO LOAD MOAI:
1. While in a local lobby, press M to load the moai map.
2. If you want to play with friends, either use Steam Remote Play or Parsec.

P.S: If you are having any issues, just restart your game or ask in the #modding-questions channel.
