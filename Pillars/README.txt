SETUP:
1. Put the 'pillarsbundle' file with no extension in your Assets folder.
2. Put the 'Pillars.dll' in your Mods folder.

HOW TO LOAD PILLARS:
1. While in a local lobby, press P to load the Pillars map.
2. If you want to play with friends, either use Steam Remote Play or Parsec.

P.S: If you are having any issues, just restart your game or ask in the #modding-questions channel.
